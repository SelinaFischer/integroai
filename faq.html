Frequently Asked Questions

Clarity for every stage of your AI journey. Find answers to common questions about AI strategy, implementation, and working with IntegroAI.

What types of organisations do you support?

+

We support leaders, startups, and SMEs that want to use AI responsibly and effectively.

Our clients are typically:

  • Executive teams looking to build AI strategy and capability across their organisation
  • Startups seeking to integrate AI into their product or operations
  • SMEs ready to implement AI solutions but lacking internal expertise
  • Organisations that have tried AI but struggled with adoption, ROI, or implementation

We work across industries including professional services, healthcare, finance, technology, and education. What matters most is your readiness to approach AI with clarity, structure, and a commitment to measurable outcomes.

What results can I expect?

+

You can expect clarity on strategy, better decisions about AI opportunities, improved team capability, and measurable progress.

Specific outcomes our clients achieve:

  • Strategic clarity: A clear AI roadmap aligned with business goals, typically delivered within 2-4 weeks
  • Better decision-making: Confidence in knowing which AI opportunities to pursue (and which to avoid)
  • Team capability: Your team gains practical AI knowledge through workshops, training, and hands-on implementation support
  • Measurable progress: Documented results including time savings, cost reductions, or revenue improvements within 3-6 months

Typical metrics include 20-40% efficiency gains in targeted processes, 30-60 day reduction in decision cycles, and documented ROI within the first implementation phase.

Do I need technical knowledge?

+

No. IntegroAI simplifies AI for leaders and teams. You only need readiness to explore what is possible.

We work with executives and teams who may have limited technical background. Our approach focuses on:

  • Business outcomes first: We translate technical concepts into business value
  • Plain language: No jargon, no assumptions about prior AI knowledge
  • Practical learning: Hands-on training that builds confidence through real examples
  • Human-centered implementation: Solutions designed for your team's actual skill level

What you do need: curiosity about AI's potential, willingness to learn, and commitment to following a structured approach. We handle the technical complexity so you can focus on strategic decisions and business impact.

How does the Discovery call work?

+

A thirty-minute conversation to understand your goals and challenges. You will receive clear guidance and practical next steps.

During the Discovery Call, we'll discuss:

  • Your current situation: Where you are with AI today (even if you're just starting)
  • Your goals: What you're trying to achieve and why AI might help
  • Your challenges: Obstacles, concerns, or past experiences with AI
  • Immediate opportunities: Quick wins we can identify based on your context
  • Next steps: Whether that's a strategy session, assessment, or implementation support

By the end of the call, you'll have a clear understanding of whether IntegroAI is the right fit, what approach makes sense for your situation, and what the timeline and investment would look like. No obligation, no pressure—just honest guidance.

Do you work with specific AI tools?

+

We prioritise strategy and strong foundations. Tools are recommended only when your needs and goals are clear.

Our tool-agnostic approach means:

  • Strategy first: We define what you need before recommending how to achieve it
  • Best fit, not trends: Tools are selected based on your specific requirements, budget, and team capability
  • No vendor lock-in: We're not tied to any specific platform or technology provider
  • Integration focus: Solutions that work with your existing systems and workflows

We work with leading AI platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, Google Gemini, Microsoft Copilot), automation tools (Make, Zapier), and custom solutions when needed. The technology serves your strategy—not the other way around.

Do you offer training?

+

Yes. We offer executive sessions, team workshops, and use case training.

Our training programs include:

  • Executive sessions: Strategic AI overview for leadership teams (2-4 hours). Focus on opportunity assessment, risk management, and building organizational AI capability.
  • Team workshops: Practical AI training for operational teams (half-day or full-day). Hands-on learning with real use cases from your business.
  • Use case training: Role-specific AI implementation training. Covers specific tools, workflows, and best practices for your team's daily work.
  • Custom programs: Tailored training designed around your industry, tools, and maturity level.

All training includes practical exercises, real examples, and post-session support materials. We can deliver in-person or remotely, depending on your preference.

What is your approach to implementation?

+

Structured. Responsible. Guided. We support you through pilots, process design, vendor supervision, and adoption.

Our implementation methodology includes:

  • Pilots: Start small with controlled tests (4-8 weeks). Validate assumptions, measure results, and refine before scaling.
  • Process design: Map current workflows, identify improvement opportunities, and design AI-enhanced processes that your team can actually use.
  • Vendor/tool supervision: If you're working with technology providers, we ensure solutions align with your strategy and protect your interests.
  • Adoption support: Change management, training, and ongoing guidance to ensure your team actually uses the solutions we implement.

Every implementation includes clear success metrics, regular progress reviews, and iterative refinement. We don't disappear after deployment—we stay engaged until you see measurable results.

Do you work remotely?

+

Yes. We support clients globally through remote consulting, with in-person options available for UK-based clients when needed.

Our remote delivery includes:

  • Video consultations: Strategy sessions, workshops, and training delivered via Zoom, Teams, or Google Meet
  • Asynchronous support: Shared documents, recorded walkthroughs, and email/Slack support between live sessions
  • Project collaboration: Cloud-based tools for real-time collaboration on strategy documents, implementation plans, and results tracking
  • Flexible scheduling: Accommodating different time zones and working hours

For UK-based clients, we can arrange in-person workshops, training sessions, or kick-off meetings when face-to-face collaboration adds value. Most clients find remote delivery highly effective and more cost-efficient.

How do I get started?

+

Book a Discovery Call to begin with clarity. We will explore your goals and define the first logical steps.

The process is simple:

  • Step 1 - Book your Discovery Call: Choose a convenient time on our calendar (30 minutes, no cost)
  • Step 2 - We discuss your situation: Share your goals, challenges, and what you've tried before
  • Step 3 - Get clear recommendations: We'll outline the right approach for your situation—whether that's strategy, training, or implementation
  • Step 4 - Decide your next step: If it feels like a good fit, we'll propose a tailored engagement. If not, you'll still leave with valuable insights.

No hard sell, no obligation. Just honest guidance on whether AI can help your business and what the smartest path forward looks like.
